Custom Models

We can create an Excel model custom tailored to fit your deal your and requirements.

We believe that 'one size fits all' creates unnecessary complexity and in turn introduces added risk to your deal. By creating a model tailor made for your deal we can increase the likelihood that its output will accurately reflect the reality of the deal.

Scenario Analysis

We understand that assumptions are just that - assumptions, and there is no one exact picture of the future. We can work with you to tweak your assumptions giving you a range of possible outcomes and a more realistic analysis.

We can also help on the front end, assisting you in structuring the deal in the most beneficial way. Got a few term sheets? Let us help you decide which one will give you the best return.

Investor Relations

If requested, we can review the model in detail with your investors and answer any financial questions about the deal they might have.
